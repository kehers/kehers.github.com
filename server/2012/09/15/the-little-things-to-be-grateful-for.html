The little things to be grateful for

15 Sep 2012

Tuesday 11. Came home late to the gist that a snake was sighted in the house. As it was late and we couldn't run any thorough search, I had to go pass the night at a friend's.

Thursday 13. Had a minor bike accident. Sustained few bruises on both legs.

Appetite.
The warmth of your bed.
Sleeping with both eyes closed.
Feeling the rush of wind across your face as you go over 80km/h.
Friends.
Neighbours.

There are a lot of little things to be grateful for.

And oh, the internet too :)
