High School Students

We work with the parents of students as young as 14 and begin having the ‘what do you want to do with your life’ conversation. We believe that starting early enables a seed to be planted and nurtured, shaping the students’ thoughts about education and career aspirations

School Leavers

It is usually at this point where young adults reach a cross road and have to make a decision on what they want to pursue. Sakamanje works with this group to advise, guide and nurture their decisions based on facts, skills, experience and market demans

University Students

Sakamanje believes that this is the subset of Students that requires close assistance and guidance as you make up a large percentage of the future African work force. It is important that the investment that you have made to educate yourselves to this level sees a return.

Don't want to go to the university?

We help you explore alternative routes into the professions. Use Sakamanje’s careers adviser to get advice about your career choices.
