About Us

Based in Accra, Ghana; Sakamanje is an educational Consultancy Agency dedicated to providing innovative solutions and support for parents and young adults. We are passionate about proving high quality independent careers advice, bridging the gap between learning and employment thus creating better life chances for young people and adults.

We are passionate about proving high quality independent careers advice, bridging the gap between learning and employment...

We work together with Public and Private Organisaions to promotes a pro-active mind set geared towards development for both teachers and students, whilst also creating and marketing employment opportunities for the students upon completion of their studies.

Sakamanje considers it necessary to engage all stakeholders in this industry (students, teachers, law makers and employers) simultaneously to enable the cultural shift towards more informed career decision making that encourages accelerated development for Ghana and Africa as a Nation.

Our Values

Making a Difference

Sakamanje strongly believes in shared values and our most important value is to make a positive impact on the livelihoods of our clients and that of our community.

Continuous Dev.

Sakamanje seeks to play a role in contributing to the growth and development of the youth. We strive to be that launch pad required for success for the young and ambitious to propel themselves and go on to accommodate to the needs of our nation.

Synergy

Sakamanje believed that Joint actions brought together increase each other's effectiveness.

Integrity

Working with the youth means we play an influential role and with that comes the responsibility to model the best. It is of utmost importance to us to always practice honesty and integrity in our choices, all of the time.
