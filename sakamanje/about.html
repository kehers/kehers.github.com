About Us

Sakamanje provides a range of packages that assist students as young as fourteen to begin thinking about their careers and provides them with the tools and support to aid them in achieving their career aspirations.

Sakamanje provides a range of packages that assist students as young as fourteen to begin thinking about their careers and provides them with the tools and support to aid them in achieving their career aspirations. Sakamanje also works together with Government Bodies and Private Organisations to create an educational curriculum which promotes a pro-active mindset towards career development for both teachers and students, while also creating and marketing employment opportunities for the students after they complete their education. Sakamanje considers it necessary to engage all stakeholders in this industry (students, teachers, law makers and employers) simultaneously to enable the cultural shift towards more informed career decision.

Sakamanje’s services are broken down into three categories

High School Students

We work with the parents of students as young as 14 and begin having the ‘what do you want to do with your life’ conversation. We believe that starting early enables a seed to be planted and nurtured, shaping the students’ thoughts about education and career aspirations.

School Leavers

It is usually at this point where young adults reach a cross road and have to make a decision on what they want to pursue. Sakamanje works with this group to advise, guide and nurture their decisions based on facts, skills, experience and market demans.

University Students – Sakamanje believes that this is the subset of Students that requires close assistance and guidance as you make up a large percentage of the future African work force. It is important that the investment that you have made to educate yourselves to this level sees a return. Sakamanje aims to map individuals skills and interests to a career that is in demand. Sakamanje also aims to assist University students to build upon the theories you are taught with solid practical experience that will differentiate you when it comes to securing employment.

Govt, Private Organisations & Educational Institutes

At Sakamanje, we believe that an eco system needs be created in order to cultivate the best results for both individuals and society as a whole. That is why we work with Govt, Private Sector and Educational Institutes to also advise on what they can do to educate and support their students. Sakamanje aims to work with the necessary partners to introduce a new curriculum that focuses on life skills, career development and regional development.
