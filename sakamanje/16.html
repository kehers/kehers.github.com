Do you know what your dream job is and how you can get it?

For school or college students and leavers this is the place to find information, advice, articles, and links to other resources to inspire you to achieve your dream career.

Whatever your age, Sakamanje will help you plan for your future. It's good to start thinking early and we're here to help you do that.

Our services include:

  1. Hourly consultation - The first hour is free and this will constitute as the initial consultation to get acquainted and for us to understand your needs

  2. Career Mapping - We help you to map your career by illustrating it. From the map, detailed information will be a result and this information will facilitate you with choices, based on individual talent and market needs. Like a GPS in your car, Career Mapping can display alternative routes to build mastery in the core professions. Mastery is being the “best you can be” and those who achieve mastery of their professions or trades are leaders, mentors and innovators. The knowledge, skills and ability that mastery requires is enduring and guides both simple day-to-day decisions as well as complex challenges. Following this exercise, Sakamanje guarantees that you will know exactly what you want to achieve and how to go about doing so.

  3. Course selection - "Do I have to take a another year of maths or can I start a second foreign language instead, which is what I really want to do?"
    "I’d like to be a Doctor, but that means I won’t have time for a Chemistry class. Will that hurt my chances of getting in?"
    Many high school seniors and junior (and their parents) want to know what courses to take to improve their chances for admission to highly competitive Universities. With the caveat that every situation is different, Sakamanje will help you assess your situation against the options so that you can make the best informed decision for your career.

  4. Work Experience Application Assistance - Doing unpaid work experience, can bring you great rewards. Work experience gives you the chance to learn new skills and improve those you already have. You will be helping others at the same time...and helping yourself build a better future. Many people are offered employment on the back of the work experience they've done - and you can join them.

Sakamanje helps you choose the organisation or group you wish to help carefully. Sakamanje believes to get the most out of your experience, it should be something you feel is important, something you feel passionately about and most importantly, that it is something that make sure you will improve your own chances of getting back into paid work.
