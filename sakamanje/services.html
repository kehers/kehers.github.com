Course Selection

It is very important that you find out as much as you can about a course before putting it down as a preference at school, University or if it is a Professional Qualification. The choices you make determine your career.

Maybe you already have a clear idea of what you would like to study. On the other hand, perhaps you are finding it difficult to decide.

Do your parents want you to study particular subjects? Have your friends told you that one course is better than another? Are you totally confused?

Let Sakamanje assist you, we not only look at your grades, but we also assess your key strengths, what you are naturally good at and help you to come to an informed decision
