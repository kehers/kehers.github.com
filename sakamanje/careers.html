Sakamanje Careers

What you do matters

Make it count Sakamanje aims to help young adults achieve their ambitions in the right way – and where better place to start other than at Sakamanje.

Sakamanje is built upon four core values Integrity, Continuous Development, Making a Difference and Unity. Our values are embedded in the way we work internally which influences the way we deliver externally. Being a part of Sakamanje means challenging the status quo, it means pushing boundaries, it means enabling our clients to excel so that our communities, our societies and in turn our nation excel. Being a part of Sakamanje means being a part of change; if this sounds like you then we would like to hear from you.

A career with us means being part of an organisation with a clear vision and strong, shared values. We’ll give you everything you need to do a great job, and your contributions will be properly recognised, highly valued and well rewarded.

It is important to note, Sakamanje is not for the faint hearted; we strive for excellence. Apply if you are ready to contribute whole-heartedly and in turn, Sakamanje will work to build you up to be the best you can be.
