Here is a list of my Android (LG Optimus black, Android 2.3.4) apps and games.

Default Intents

Music: Songbird. Strong points: simple interface and ability to manage playlist from locked screen.

Browsing: Opera Mini. Strong points: Low data consumption. I may say being used to it on other platforms is another too.

Video: MX player. Strong points: Gestures. With a simple slide I can forward/rewind, increase/decrease volume, increase/decrease brightness. Plays a wide range of video formats too.
