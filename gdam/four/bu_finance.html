GDL Finance and Leasing Company Limited (GDL Finance) is a subsidiary of Growth and Development Asset Management Limited (GDAML) with the parent company having about 90% equity stake. GDL Finance was incorporated to operate within the middle tier of Nigeria Financial system by providing financial services to individual consumers and enterprises with strong focus on Micro, small and medium enterprise segment of the economy.

At GDL Finance we visualize leasing and provision of the services of a finance company as an opportunity to provide platforms for meeting the financial needs of individual consumers and micro, small and medium enterprises to further boost economic activities. In addition to this, we also aim at achieving the followings:

  • Ensuring sustainability of financial performance

  • Improvement in income and wealth creation of all stakeholders

  • Employment generation

  • Affordable loans and other asset financing products

  • Growing and developing entrepreneurs

  • Expanding the middle class

  • Deepening the level of financial inclusion in the economy

GDL Finance and Leasing Company Limited in its quest to expand the middle class of the society as well as grow and develop small and medium scale enterprises is poised to deploy excellent, suitable and affordable financial services within the ambits of the CBN regulations towards creating value for our clientele base. The offerings that would cater for our clients’ needs include but is not limited to the following;

  • Consumer loans

  • Social loans

  • Education loans

  • High Yield Fund Management Service

  • Import and Export finance

  • Hire Purchase

  • Project finance and management

  • Debt factoring and Securitization

  • Debt administration

  • LPO financing

  • Invoice Discounting

  • Receivables Re-financing
