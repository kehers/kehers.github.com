Growth and Development Asset Management (GDL) is a company licensed by the Securities & Exchange Commission (SEC) to manage financial and non-financial assets (infrastructure) through collective investment schemes/specialized vehicles as well as provide unique and personalized investment products and services in order enable our clients to meet their distinctive investment objectives and aspirations.

Our investment management team offers discretionary and non-discretionary investment management and client advisory services for specific high net worth clients. These services are unique to each individual and designed to meet their specific investment needs. Each individual portfolio is constructed after a thorough assessment of his/her risk/return profile to ensure that the portfolio aligns with his/her objectives.
