Career

We take pride in the fact that our people are not peripheral but are at the core of our existence and thus provide them an enabling environment of open communication, teamwork, career fulfillment and continuous development.

GDL as a company with an unwavering commitment to excellence and the highest level of professionalism offers great career opportunities for ambitious and high performing individuals.

We have an open work environment ensconced in a culture and value system of teamwork, audacity, integrity, result-orientation and strong social conscience.

Our people are not mere appendages, they are our most valued asset working in a place with immense opportunities for career advancement, coupled with a very competitive employment benefits on offer in Nigeria's financial services landscape.

Working together as a team of seasoned professionals using a combination of meticulous and result oriented financial management skills to engender the sustainable growth and development of our diverse assets portfolios makes working with us a career enriching experience.

If you are aiming for 'more beyond' then you fit our ideal candidate. Send your resume to humanresources@gdl.com.ng
