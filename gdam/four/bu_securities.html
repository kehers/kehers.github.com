GDL Securities Limited is a wholly owned subsidiary of Growth and Development Limited (GDL) and duly licensed by both The Nigerian Stock Exchange and Securities and Exchange Commission to carry our stockbroking business in Nigeria. It is steered by a crop of young and committed professionals with collective experience of over five decades in the Nigerian Capital Market.

At GDL Securities Limited, we allow high level of INTEGRITY to reflect in every business dealing and the value of INVESTOR’S FIRST in investment decisions and advisory services to all the clients.

We provide best execution service to clients who are well informed in relation to asset classes like equities, fixed income instruments, currency etc. and advisory services to clients requesting for well-researched information to make sound investment decision. Our advisory service is supported by robust research unit equipped with experienced professionals and state of the art technology to monitor both local and international financial markets. Our processes are structured to ensure timely and quality service delivery to our clients.

Our other value adding services/product offerings are:

  • Portfolio Management for equities only

  • Portfolio Management for fixed income instrument only targeted at clients interested in guaranteed income and capital preservation. We have expertise in money market instruments – Treasury Bills, Commercial Paper etc. and capital market instruments – FGN bonds, non-sovereign bonds and corporate bonds with good credit quality

  • Advisory service for optimal mix of equity and fixed income instruments to meet investment objectives of large spectrum of investors ranging from risk averse to risk taking.

  • Facility that allows clients to have on-line access to their statement of account and stock positions.

  • Liquidity management & enhancement.

  • Share Certificate Management

  • Dividend Warrant Management

  • Nominal Transfer

  • Share Transmission
